# Need Gone Vision

## Mission

Make giving effortless. Make getting exciting.

Need Gone helps people give away useful items locally without the hassle of selling, haggling, or endless messaging.

## Core Idea

Need Gone is not just a free marketplace.

It is a structured giving platform.

The app should reduce the work of giving as much as possible through simple clicks, structured pickup agreements, expiration rules, and reputation.

## What Makes Need Gone Different

- No selling
- No haggling
- No unnecessary messaging
- Structured pickup agreements
- Pickup privacy options
- Reputation based on real behavior
- Listings expire instead of sitting forever
- Givers can offer items to trusted groups first

## North Star

Reduce the work of giving.

Every feature should answer:

Does this make it easier for someone to give something away?

## Product Principles

### 1. Giving should be easy

A giver should be able to post an item in under 60 seconds.

### 2. Getting should feel exciting

Claimers should enjoy browsing because they might win something useful for free.

### 3. Messaging should not be required

The app should replace back-and-forth messages with structured choices, buttons, pickup windows, and agreements.

### 4. Privacy matters

Some givers are comfortable with porch pickup.
Some only want public meetups.
Need Gone must support both.

### 5. Reputation should be fact-based

Avoid star ratings at first.
Track real behavior:

- Completed pickups
- Missed pickups
- Items given
- Items claimed
- Canceled before pickup

### 6. The app manages the lifecycle

Need Gone should help expire listings, handle missed pickups, offer to next requester, and reduce manual follow-up.

## Target Users

### Givers

People who have useful items they want gone quickly with minimal hassle.

Examples:

- Parents clearing out kids items
- People moving
- Homeowners decluttering
- People replacing furniture
- People who would rather give than sell

### Claimers

People who enjoy finding useful free items locally.

Examples:

- Families
- Students
- DIYers
- Bargain hunters
- People in need
- People who enjoy treasure hunting

## Core Promise

Post it.
Set the pickup rules.
Approve a requester.
Need Gone handles the rest.
